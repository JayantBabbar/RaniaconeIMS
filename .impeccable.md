# RaniacOne — Design Context

## Users
B2B buyers and operators evaluating an inventory management system for mid-market warehousing. Two audiences in one page: the operator who'll live in it daily (wants to feel the system is fast and trustworthy) and the IT/ops decision-maker (wants enterprise-grade signal — RBAC, audit trail, multi-tenant isolation, FIFO valuation). They land here from a sales touch or search for "warehouse management software" and need to be convinced in under 30 seconds that this is a serious system, not a toy.

## Brand Personality
**Precise · Kinetic · Methodical.** A working warehouse never stops, but every move is logged. The product feels confident, not clever. It moves a lot of weight without making noise about it. Comparable peers in tone: Stripe (technical confidence), Linear (precision aesthetic), Ramp (enterprise polish without stuffiness).

## Aesthetic Direction
**Modern bento on a white-blue palette.** Light theme. Asymmetric composition. The hero is text-left + video-right (not full-bleed). On scroll, a bento "journey" panel rises over the hero — a 3D truck (real glb model) drives down a center road, bento tiles flank either side telling the system's story stop-by-stop. Side animations sync to the truck's vertical position via GSAP ScrollTrigger.

**Anti-references:** No gradient text. No `border-left: 4px solid` accent stripes. No glowing cyan-on-dark "AI SaaS" look. No glassmorphism. No identical 3-column feature grid. No purple-to-blue gradients.

**Differentiator:** The bento *is* the road. Most bentos are static; here the tiles literally line a highway and reveal as the truck reaches them.

## Design Principles
1. **Asymmetry over symmetry.** Bento tiles vary in size (1×1, 1×2 wide, 2×1 tall). The hero is split 60/40, not 50/50.
2. **One brand color, used precisely.** Monochromatic blue. The truck (real photo/3D) carries the only warm element in frame.
3. **Motion is choreography, not decoration.** Every animation is anchored to scroll progress or a real state change. No idle-loop sparkle.
4. **Reduced motion is a first-class state.** With `prefers-reduced-motion`, the page works as a static, beautifully-typeset bento page.
5. **Professional > playful.** Bold copy, restrained ornament. The truck does the playing; the typography stays calm.
